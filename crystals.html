Crystals

Below you will see some photos of various crystals I have grown during high school. Some took only several weeks, while others took months. Patience is the key - setting it up and forgeting about it for a few weeks is best. Additionally, remembering to replenish the solution every once in a while and break off any potential defect crystals helps a lot.
