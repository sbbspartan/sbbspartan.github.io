Welcome

My name is Samuel Belden, and I am a Materials Engineer at SpaceX working on providing high speed, low latency internet to the world with Starlink. I graduated from MIT sin 2018 with a Bachelor's Degree in Materials Science and Engineering. I'm primarily interested in mechanical design, polymer science, and aerospace. My focus in Materials Engineering so far has been in product design, adhesives and polymers design/testing/selection, process engineering, materials reliability testing, and in bringing up mass production consumer electronics. I was involved in various materials science projects and research in my time at MIT, ranging from lab work to hands-on prototyping and manufacturing.

I enjoy hands-on, challenging engineering projects. I've built my own electron microscope that can 'see' atoms, made custom injection molded yo-yo's, designed and built my own carbon fiber bike, along with various other projects. I'm always up to something.

Outside of work, I'm an avid backpacker, camper, hiker, rock climber, canoer, rower, cook, baker, and entrepreneur.

I extensively used NX CAD, ANSYS mechanical, thermal, and electromagnetic simulation software, and Matlab.
